# AI INTERN

AI INTERN is a web-based AI-powered internship guidance platform designed to help students explore career opportunities, identify skill gaps, and prepare for internships through personalized recommendations and interactive AI tools.

The platform combines internship discovery, a custom recommendation engine, the **Tejaswini AI Career Assistant**, resume screening, and a mock interview simulator into a single user-friendly experience.

## Features

### Internship Categories
Browse internship opportunities across multiple industries, including:
- IT & Software Development
- Banking & Financial Services
- Healthcare
- Manufacturing
- Education
- Telecom
- Agriculture
- And many more.

### Tejaswini – AI Career Assistant
An AI-powered career guidance chatbot integrated through **Hugging Face Spaces** that helps students:
- Answer career-related questions
- Recommend learning paths
- Identify skill gaps
- Suggest technologies to learn
- Guide students toward suitable internship opportunities

### Internship Recommendation System
A custom-built recommendation engine developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that:
- Collects user education level and existing skills
- Calculates internship compatibility
- Displays the top matching internships
- Shows match percentages, stipends, locations, and required skills
- Redirects beginners to the AI Career Assistant for personalized guidance

### Resume Screening
Upload or paste your resume to receive AI-powered feedback on:
- Skills
- Resume structure
- Missing sections
- Improvement suggestions
- Role-specific recommendations

### Mock Interview Simulator (Beta)
Practice role-specific interviews with an AI-powered mock interview simulator that provides personalized feedback to improve interview performance.

> **Note:** This feature is currently in beta and is actively being improved. Occasional inconsistencies may occur.

### Success Stories
Explore inspiring career journeys to motivate and guide aspiring students.

### Contact Page
A custom-built contact form that allows users to submit queries and feedback directly.
